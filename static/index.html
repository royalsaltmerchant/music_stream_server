FRC Radio

On-demand music streaming for your group

How It Works

1. Create a Channel

Enter a channel name below and click "Create Channel" to open your host control panel.

2. Choose Music

Select a playlist from the host panel to start streaming music to your channel.

3. Share with Listeners

Copy the listener link from your host panel and send it to your friends. They can open it in any browser to listen in sync.

Create Your Channel

Tip: Channel names can only contain letters, numbers, hyphens, and underscores. Spaces will be converted to hyphens automatically.
